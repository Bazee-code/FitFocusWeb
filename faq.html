Frequently Asked Questions

How does StepFocus work?

StepFocus blocks selected apps until you've met your daily health goal as set by the user. Once you reach your goal, the apps unlock automatically.

Can I set custom health goals?

Yes! You can set personalized health goals in your app settings depending on your daily activity targets.

What apps can I block?

You can block any installed app or even entire app categories. This feature is fully customizable based on your needs.

Does StepFocus work offline?

Yes. Since we use the Apple Healthkit data to compare with user defined goals, app restrictions continue to work even if you're offline. The data syncs once you're back online.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. All your health data and app usage stats are stored locally and never shared with third parties.
